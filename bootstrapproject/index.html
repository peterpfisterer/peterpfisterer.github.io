About Us

How We Started







Our company started as a ski house in Revelstoke, British Columbia. We have always tried our hardest to provide our customers with the highest quality backcountry and outdoor gear on the market. This philosophy quickly led to our expansion and now our stores are in every province and 28 states!

Our Values







Our non-profit has always been committed to the customer, but we are also committed to change. Three years ago we decided to donate 10% of our profits to charity. We also sell products made by companies that use a sustainable business model. How can we claim to care if we endorse brands that do not?

Commitment to the Customer







Our commitment to the customer means more than just deals and free returns. We believe that in order to enjoy the outdoors, you need the right equipment and outerwear to allow you to do so. That is why we only sell quality equipment, and have a knowledgable customer support staff to assist you with any of your needs.
