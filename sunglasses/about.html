About our business:

Here at Sosa Glasses, we believe in not only making the world a better place through our sunglasses, but also through our various charities.

When you buy a pair of Sosa Glasses, you can rest assured knowing that your purchase is funding various charities around the world. We as a company believe in giving back, so we decided that a whopping 0.5% of the money made from each pair of glasses sold will be going to charity.

On top of our various charities, we also provide our workers with the highest-end suicide nets surrounding our sweatshops. In doing this, we have cut fatalities on the job by 15%!

When you buy something from a company, you give them your support. That is why we like to be completely transparent with our customers. If you have any inquiries, please direct yourself to our contact page.
